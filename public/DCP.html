Program of CareDementia Care

Villa Monticello is a Licensed Residential Care Facility for the Elderly that recognizes that caring for a resident with Dementia is not a “one size fits all." Our dementia care program is designed to focus and meet the personal and unique needs of our residents.

Our Goals

  • To develop and implement a effective dementia program and plan of care which will enhance the quality of life and meed the individualized needs of the residents we served
  • To forge a collaborative and close working partnership between the staff , family, friends and medical professional towards achieving a common goal.
  • To create an ongoing staff dementia training so they will be equipped with adequate knowledge regarding proper care of Dementia

    • 5 Point Strategy of Dementia Program (C.A.R.E.S)

  • C - Careful assessment of each resident’s unique and individual needs
  • A - Adapt and implement a program/service plan developed to meet the ever changing needs of our residents
  • R - Review and assess on a regular basis the program and plan of care implemented to evaluate its effectiveness and relevance
  • E - Empower the residents with dementia and the staff of caregivers , families, friends and medical professional by building a positive partnership toward achieving a common goal
  • S - Secure a safe environment essential to the total well being of our residents
