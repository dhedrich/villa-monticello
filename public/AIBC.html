All Levels of CareAll-Inclusive Basic Services

Direct Care, Assistance and Monitoring

  • 24 hour care of monitoring, supervision and assistance as needed
  • Assist with showers and daily hygiene including shaving
  • Assist with hearing aids and eye glasses and other appliance
  • Toileting and incontinent care
  • Night monitoring and assistance as needed
  • Observation and reporting of any abnormal conditions to the primary care physician and family/responsible party
  • 1 person minimal assist with transfer and mobility
  • Minor wound /skin care
  • Coordinate and work with outside services as per Doctor’s order such as home health, hospice care, physical and occupational therapy..
  • Schedule medical and dental appointments and arrange transportation upon request
  • Hospice Care
  • Call 911 or emergency transport in case of any incidence that will require emergency treatment. Cost to be paid by Resident’s responsible party

Meals Management

  • 3 Nutritious meals plus snacks . Unlimited beverages not unless contraindicated
  • Escort to and from meals and activities as needed
  • Assistance and monitoring of meals and diet as prescribed by physician
  • Modified diet as per Doctor’s order ( ex. Low salt , low sugar, soft or pureed)
  • Observe and report any changes in appetite and ability to swallow or allergies to food.
  • Encourage fluid intake except when contraindicated

Medication Management

  • Medications centrally stored and locked in a med cart
  • Properly dispense medicines as per Doctors order by trained med tech
  • Proper inventory and documentation of medications including narcotics
  • In charge with order of new meds and refills of routine and PRN medications
  • Proper destruction of discontinued and expired medications
  • Observation and reporting of possible allergic reactions and bad side effects of medications given

Social, Mental and Emotional Support and Behavior Management

  • Stimulating activities that promote wellness, mental stimulation and socialization
  • Observe and report any changes in moods and behavior, mental capacity and emotional status
  • Managing “ sundowning” behavior by redirecting and providing closer attention and supervision

Housekeeping, Laundry Services and Basic Supplies and Furnishing Included

  • Basic housekeeping
  • Basic toiletries supplies
  • Laundry service for regular clothes . no dry clean and no washing of fine clothes
  • Basic toiletries and hygiene supplies
  • Incontinent Supplies
  • Basic bedroom furnitures ( twin size bed, side table, lamp, chair , Dresser or closet
  • Linens, sheets, pillows, blankets included
