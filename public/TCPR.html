Total Care Pachage Rate Sheet

How We Charge

We understand that caring for a resident with Dementia is not a “One Size Fits All” . With that in mind , we offer range of prices based on your choice of accommodation and the assistance and supervision required for each level of care.. Furthermore, to minimize financial stress , we designed our rate to be All Inclusive of the basic care and supplies needed by each resident.

To determine the actual rate, we created a simple process where we carefully assess the resident’s current condition and his needs for care and supervision. After the assessment, a private conference with the family is done to discuss and finalize the reasonable cost within the price range set on our scheduled fees and additional * special care needs fees , if applicable.

Scheduled Fees

Villa 1 and Main Villa

  • Shared Room $4500 - $4950
  • Private Room $5000- $6000

Villa 2 and 3

  • Shared Room $4000-4500
  • Private Room $4500- 4900

** Rates are guidelines and may be subject to change and modification after assessment and evaluation of care needed.

Range in prices are based on the special care needs required on top of the basic needs and services

Special Care Needs is designed for those who has other related conditions and limitations that demand additional and individualized care, personal attention and closer supervision.
